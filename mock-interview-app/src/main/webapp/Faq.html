Frequently Asked Questions

Why does the "request an interview link" disappear after I request an interview?

At the moment each user can only request one interview at a time.

I want to be the interviewer but I don't see a button to accept an interview request on the listings page. How do I accept one?

From the listings page, you can click on a listing that interests you, and a page with the full interview request details will appear. On that page, there will be a button to accept an interview request.

Where will the mock interview take place?

The mock interview will take place on the coding environment from the interview listing. Communicating will be done over the Google Hangout link also from the interview listing.

Does the Video Chat link have to be through Google Hangouts?

As of now yes.

During the interview, should I have my camera on?

It is up to you because most interviews are over the phone.

What's the benefit of giving an interview?

Giving an interview can help you increase your understanding of that type of (algorithm/data structure) question. It will also make you more aware of what interviewers look for when your the interviewee.
